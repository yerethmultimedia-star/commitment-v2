# Commitment Product Vision

## Mission

> Help people transform meaningful intentions into lasting habits through commitments they actually fulfill.

## Vision

> Become the most trusted personal commitment platform, helping users build an identity aligned with their long‑term goals rather than simply tracking tasks.

## Target Audience

- Professionals pursuing long‑term goals.
- Students.
- People building new habits.
- Anyone who repeatedly abandons personal objectives.

## Core Problems

- People lose motivation after the initial excitement.
- Existing habit trackers emphasize streaks instead of identity.
- Goals are often disconnected from daily action.
- Most productivity apps do not adapt when users struggle.

## Core Principles

- **Identity before tasks** – commitments shape identity.
- **Progress over perfection** – small wins matter more than flawless streaks.
- **Small consistent actions** – encourage daily micro‑steps.
- **Reflection over punishment** – learn from setbacks.
- **Privacy by default** – user data stays private.
- **Offline by design** – core features work without connectivity.
- **AI assists but never decides** – AI offers suggestions, users retain control.

## North Star

**Commitment Transformation Index (CTI)**

The Commitment Transformation Index operationalizes the outcomes defined in Chapter 9 of the Framework (`docs/00-framework/THE_COMMITMENT_FRAMEWORK.md`). It is not a new product philosophy, but the primary measurement model derived from it.

The current implementation of the Commitment Transformation Index evaluates multiple dimensions of user transformation, including — but not limited to:

- increasing personalization of Blueprints,
- healthy adaptation,
- decreasing system dependence,
- longitudinal evidence of internalized change.

**No operational metric may replace the Commitment Transformation Index as the North Star unless the Framework itself is deliberately amended.**

## Operational KPIs

- Percentage of active commitments successfully completed.

## Health Metrics

- Adoption rate of new users.
- Retention after 30 days.
- Average number of active commitments per user.
- User‑reported satisfaction (NPS).

## Design Principles

- Clean, calm UI with high contrast and readability.
- Minimal friction onboarding.
- Accessible interactions for all abilities.
- Consistent visual language that reinforces identity.

## Out of Scope (v1)

- Social features and public commitments.
- Marketplace or monetization.
- Team or group commitments.
- Third‑party integrations (e.g., wearables).
- AI Coach autonomy.

## Long‑term Evolution

- Expand AI coaching with personalized habit recommendations.
- Introduce community challenges while preserving privacy.
- Integrate with calendars, task managers, and wearables.
- Offer premium analytics dashboards.
- Support multi‑user/team commitments for projects and families.
